Context Count Threshold


Threshold: The context threshold is the amount of context you believe is taken under consideration by ChatGPT
Context Weight: The weight given to the word count in calculating the context count. For example, a weight of 75% means that the context count is 75% of the word count.
Warning Threshold: The warning threshold for the context count, expressed as a percentage of the threshold value. For example, a threshold of 80% means that the warning threshold is 80% of the threshold value.
